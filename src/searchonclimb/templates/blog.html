How to Prepare for a Trek?

1. Physical Fitness

Prepare with cardio, strength training, and endurance exercises before the trek.

2. Proper Gear & Packing

Essential trekking gear includes:

  • Sturdy trekking shoes
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • A comfortable backpack
  • Hydration and energy snacks

3. Mental Preparation

Trekking is as much a mental challenge as a physical one. Stay positive and embrace the journey.
